Welcome to Trustworthy AI

AI is transforming everything

As Artificial Intelligence (AI) is becoming a widely used tool in various fields, adequate methods have to be provided to allow users to employ AI techniques effectively. However, AI still faces prolonged challenges with low user acceptance as well as seeing system disuse, misuse, or even failure. These fundamental challenges can be attributed to the lack of user trust in AI. Blockchain technology provides benefits of security, decentralization, privacy and transparency. We incorporate the blockchain technology into AI to allow secure evaluations of AI models with data around the world by using the Ethereum Blockchain and smart contracts, and thereby boosting trustworthy AI. Both AI model owners and data owners get rewards from blockchain supported AI evaluations.
